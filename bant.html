BANT is a proven framework to help sales teams focus on leads that are most likely to convert. It stands for Budget, Authority, Need, and Timeline four signals that tell you when a prospect is ready to engage.

Get more sales

What Is BANT?

Budget

Does the prospect have funds set aside for your solution?

Authority

Are you talking to the people who make or influence buying decisions?

Need

Is there a clear business problem or goal that your offering can address?

Timeline

When is the prospect looking to move forward?
